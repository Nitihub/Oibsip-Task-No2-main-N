About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, one of the greatest cricketers in the world, is celebrated for his consistency, aggressive captaincy, and unmatched work ethic. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, he has left an indelible mark on the world of cricket.

Life Highlights

  • 1988: Born in Delhi, India.
  • 2008: Captained India to victory in the U-19 World Cup.
  • 2008: Made his ODI debut for India.
  • 2014: Became the captain of the Indian Test team.
  • 2018: Fastest cricketer to score 10,000 ODI runs.
  • 2022: Memorable T20 innings against Pakistan in the World Cup.

Legacy and Contributions

Virat Kohli has revolutionized cricket with his fitness-driven approach and never-give-up attitude. A role model for aspiring athletes, his journey inspires millions worldwide.
